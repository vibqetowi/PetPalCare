Problem statement

2. PetPalCare – A Pet Caring App

Meet Alice, a dedicated office worker with an endearing canine companion. Unfortunately, her hectic work schedule and travels have made it challenging for her to fulfill her pet's needs consistently. The lack of time and energy has left Alice struggling to provide essential care like walks, playtime, feeding, and clean-up for her furry friend. In light of these challenges, Alice is on the lookout for reliable individuals who can offer assistance in caring for her pet during busy periods or when she's away from her city. Your task is to design a thoughtful mobile application that connects pet owners like Alice with trustworthy individuals who are passionate about caring for animals. This app aims to provide a reliable solution for pet care, ensuring that pets receive the attention and love they deserve even when their owners have demanding schedules or travel plans. Your app design should include the screens for pet owners like Alice finding individuals who can offer assistance as well as screens for individuals looking for gigs to take care of pets.

User Requirements and User Journey

Primary User: Alice the Busy, Tech-Savvy Professional

  • Background: Alice balances a demanding career with the love for her pet dog, seeking digital solutions to ensure her pet's well-being despite her busy schedule and travels.
  • Values: Values both her professional commitments and her pet's health, striving for a balance between work and pet care.
  • Lifestyle: Accustomed to leveraging technology for convenience, Alice looks for innovative pet care solutions during her absences.

Alice's User Journey:

  1. Profile Setup: After downloading PetPalCare, Alice quickly sets up her profile and registers her pets. She indicates her pet's information like species, size, health considerations, ect. leveraging the app's easy-to-navigate interface.
  2. Service Request:With all her information on file, Alice easily creates a request by choosing a pet (if applicable) and inputting the period in which care is needed.
  3. Automated Matching: Utilizing the app's algorithm, Alice is instantaneously matched with several highly-rated caregivers, including Bob, based on proximity and her specific care requirements.
  4. Reviewing Caregiver Profiles: Alice reviews the profiles and ratings of her matches, appreciating the transparent information on each caregiver's experience and skills.
  5. Selection and Instant Booking: Selecting Bob due to his exceptional ratings and experience with similar pets, Alice books his services with a few taps, impressed by the seamless booking experience.
  6. Real-time Updates and Communication: Throughout the care period, Alice stays updated with real-time photos and messages from Bob, ensuring her pet's well-being and peace of mind.
  7. Secure Payment and Rating: At the end of the service, Alice easily makes a secure payment through the app and leaves a review for Bob, contributing to the community-driven trust system.

Secondary User: Bob the Compassionate Pet Caregiver

  • Background: Bob is passionate about animals, seeking to care for pets and offer them love and attention in their owner's absence.
  • Values: He values the well-being and happiness of pets, aiming to provide peace of mind to pet owners through dependable care.
  • Lifestyle: Looking for flexible opportunities to connect with pets in need, Bob uses his passion for animals to earn income and fulfill his passion.

Bob's User Journey:

  1. Onboarding and Profile Verification: Bob signs up on PetPalCare and completes his profile, including passing a background check, showcasing his dedication and reliability as a caregiver.
  2. Receiving Match Notifications: Bob receives notifications for pet care opportunities that match his skills and preferences, including a request from Alice, facilitated by the app's efficient matching algorithm.
  3. Accepting Requests and Scheduling: After reviewing Alice's pet care needs, Bob accepts the request and schedules the service, appreciating the app's streamlined communication features.
  4. Providing Care and Sharing Updates: During the care period, Bob shares regular updates and photos with Alice, ensuring transparency and building trust through the app's communication platform. The app also provides him tools to help him keep track of the pet's needs.
  5. Completion and Feedback: At the end of the service, Bob receives his payment directly through the app and reads Alice's positive review, motivating him to continue offering exceptional pet care.

Solution Overview

The PetPalCare app draws inspiration from the seamless user experience pioneered by ridesharing services like Uber and Grab. Our platform streamlines the pet care search process by matching users with caregivers based on their pet's specific needs.


By leveraging advanced algorithms, PetPalCare ensures matches are made considering crucial factors like reputation, proximity, and service requirements. This automated matching system is designed to align with the lifestyles of busy pet owners like Alice, simplifying the process of finding the perfect caregiver for her pet.


With a strong emphasis on security and trust, PetPalCare includes a comprehensive verification and review system alongside innovative features like automated caregiver matching and real-time video check-ins.


These innovations form the core of our platform, aimed at creating a seamless and secure pet care experience for both owners and caregivers.

Advanced Matching System

Inspired by the convenience of ridesharing applications like Uber and Grab, PetPalCare integrates a sophisticated matching system. This feature intelligently pairs pet owners with caregivers by evaluating critical criteria such as service requirements, proximity, and availability.

The matching process unfolds as follows:

  1. Alice completes her registration and creates a comprehensive pet profile, detailing each pet's specific needs.
  2. Critical pet information is captured, including but not limited to:
    • Type of pet
    • Physical characteristics such as weight and hair length
    • Dietary preferences and nutritional needs
  3. Location services are enabled to facilitate matches based on geographic proximity.
  4. Alice selects the specific pet needing care and outlines the service duration.
  5. The system dynamically generates a list of suitable caregivers, showcasing their availability, trust scores, and any relevant certifications.
  6. Alice reviews her options, considering factors like rates and caregiver ratings, and selects her preferred caregiver.
  7. The platform seamlessly transitions Alice to a communication interface, where she can engage with the chosen caregiver.
Trust Through Transparency: Random Video Check-Ins

To bolster trust and provide peace of mind, PetPalCare introduces random video check-ins, a feature influenced by the authentic interaction model of BeReal. During care periods, caregivers are prompted to record live updates at spontaneous intervals, ensuring real-time insight into the pet's well-being. This system guarantees Alice remains informed and confident in the care her pet receives.

Operational flow:

  1. With active location and data services, Bob starts the care session, triggering the system's random timer for video check-ins.
  2. At a randomly determined moment, Bob is alerted to record a short video update. Alice can also initiate a check-in request.
  3. Accessing the notification, Bob is directed to a user-friendly interface designed for quick video capture.
  4. Limited attempts ensure the authenticity of each check-in, with the video automatically uploaded upon completion.
  5. Advanced video analysis verifies the presence of both Bob and the pet, maintaining a high standard of care verification.
  6. Upon successful upload, Alice receives a notification, providing her with immediate reassurance of her pet's status.
Chat Interface and Urgent Messaging

To facilitate direct and efficient communication, PetPalCare introduces a chat interface allowing Alice and Bob to exchange messages, request video updates, and send urgent messages that notify the receiver's phone immediately.

Features include:

  • Real-Time Messaging: Instant chat capabilities ensure Alice and Bob can communicate seamlessly throughout the care period.
  • Video Update Requests: Alice can request real-time video updates from Bob, providing peace of mind and transparency regarding her pet's well-being.
  • Urgent Messaging: Both Alice and Bob can send urgent messages, triggering notifications on the receiver's phone to highlight immediate attention needs.

This chat interface is designed to strengthen the trust between pet owners and caregivers by ensuring open lines of communication and the ability to respond swiftly to any situation.

Reflection

Throughout this course on UX/UI design, I encountered a pivotal moment of realization that profoundly shifted my perspective on design processes. Initially, I found myself grappling with Figma, a tool commonly utilized for UI design. However, my discomfort with Figma steered me towards an alternative path—creating a website. This decision was not merely a workaround; it became a journey into understanding the essence of user-centric design.

This course illuminated the importance of empathy in design. It taught me to transcend my personal preferences, focusing instead on the needs and experiences of the user. For instance, the color scheme of the app, which I developed, does not align with my personal taste. Yet, I chose it deliberately, aiming to evoke a sense of wholesomeness and friendliness. This decision was anchored in my consideration of Alice's concerns for trust and safety—attributes that would resonate with her and potentially enhance her user experience.

Another significant learning point was the realization of the innovative potential within this project. The concept of streamlining the booking process, inspired by the efficiency of platforms like Uber, underscored the importance of a seamless user experience. This approach not only addresses the specific needs of a busy individual like Alice but also showcases the potential to stand out in the market, possibly without direct competitors due to its unique value proposition.

The journey to conceptualize and design this app was challenging yet enlightening. It underscored the essence of detaching from one’s preferences to truly serve the user's needs. More so, it highlighted the importance of innovation and empathy in crafting solutions that are not only effective but also meaningful to the end user. This course has been instrumental in shaping my understanding of UX/UI design, guiding me through the intricacies of creating a user-centric digital product.
